A couple of nights ago, the baby who I'm on parental leave (our soon-to-be 11
month old) slept through the night for the first time!<!--more-->

From around 7:15pm all the way through to 6am, he didn't have a single wake up
or need any intervention. Well done that man.

This morning, he woke up at 4:30am and decided that was morning time so I was
eating poached eggs on toast at 5:50am. A good reminder that development is two
steps forward and one step back. Or sometimes, one step forward and then all
the steps back.

All the same, a major milestone. Now if only he can get it to stick...
