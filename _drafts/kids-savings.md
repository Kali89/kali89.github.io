I'm thinking about savings strategies for my children and it's all getting very
complicated. In the UK, we have a dizzying array of savings vehicles and it can
sometimes be a bit baffling, so I thought I'd sketch out what I'm thinking and
let's see if it makes sense!<!--more-->

## Taking Care of Me

My philosophy thus far has been that it's better to look after myself
financially before I look after the kids. Put your own oxygen mask on first on
the aeroplane and all that jazz. But really, it makes sense. If I'm sorted then
I can choose to pass on money or pay for things or generally make my kids lives
easier however I see fit.

I'm disciplined so I'm not worried about blowing through money that I should be
saving, so really keeping hold of money makes the most sense.
